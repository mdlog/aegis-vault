### 2 · Go‑To‑Market (±75 detik)
📝 *Marketplace dua sisi → operator‑first → trust‑first → bertahap → metrik jujur.*

🗣️ "Aegis is a **two‑sided marketplace** — AI operators on one side, depositors on the other — so we face the classic cold‑start problem. Our answer is **operator‑first**: we seed the harder side first, because a credible operator brings their own audience."

🗣️ "Our philosophy is **trust first, yield second**. We sell **verifiable, bounded risk‑management** — not 'unlimited AI returns.' Users can see the rules the contract enforces, instead of trusting a promise."

🗣️ "The sequence is staged: start with our own in‑house vault as proof the loop runs, then recruit operators who already have a track record, then bring in curators and allocators, and finally larger depositors. And we hold a strict discipline — **we measure success by depositor retention and surviving drawdowns, not by inflated headline numbers.**"

### 3 · Resources Needed (±75 detik)
📝 *Likuiditas #1 (buat actionable utk 0G) → intro → audit → compute/storage → multi‑chain dgn 0G inti.*

🗣️ "What we need most, in order. **Number one is deeper DEX liquidity on 0G.** Liquidity is the bottleneck — without depth, a vault can't operate at meaningful size. Concretely, where 0G could help: **liquidity incentives, market‑maker introductions, or a co‑incentive program** to bootstrap depth."

🗣️ "Second, **ecosystem introductions** — to credible operators, and to one risk allocator. Third, **support for a first independent security audit.** Fourth, **technical support on 0G Compute and Storage.**"

🗣️ "And one strategic point: Aegis is designed so **0G stays the core** — the AI brain, identity, and reputation live on 0G. If we ever need deeper liquidity than 0G can provide in time, **execution** can extend to other EVM chains, but that **extends 0G's reach, it doesn't leave 0G**. Deepening 0G liquidity simply keeps more of that activity on 0G."

### 4 · 0G Deep Incubation (±60 detik)
📝 *Ya → cocok alami → milestone 90 hari konkret → prove‑first.*

🗣️ "On incubation — **yes, we're very interested.** Because we're already 0G‑native, this is a natural fit, not a pivot. Almost everything we just asked for is exactly what an acceleration program provides."

🗣️ "And we'll commit to **concrete 90‑day milestones**: move governance to a real multisig and our keys to hardware signing, turn on the protocol fee loop, onboard our first few external operators, run one guarded vault, and **publish its real, on‑chain net‑PnL.**"

🗣️ "We see this as a **two‑way partnership**: 0G helps us cross to real capital, and we become a flagship example of safe, honest AI‑DeFi on 0G — built on one principle: **prove the numbers first, together.**"

- **"Does the AI actually make money?"** → 🗣️ "Honestly, not proven net‑of‑fees yet — and that's exactly what we want to demonstrate with one guarded vault, transparently on‑chain, before we scale."
- **"Why is the live vault so small / no users?"** → 🗣️ "It's a demonstration vault. The real constraint is on‑chain liquidity depth, and we've chosen not to take public funds before that's solved — which is why liquidity is our top ask."

- **"Is 'verifiable AI' real on‑chain today?"** → 🗣️ "Today it's a single signing key, not a hardware enclave proof. Making that a true on‑chain TEE attestation is on our roadmap, and it's part of the technical support we'd value from 0G."
